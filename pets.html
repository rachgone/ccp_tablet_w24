Pets

Here are some of my favourite dogs!


Lovely Lilo


Lilo is a 1-year-old American Pit Bull Terrier. She prefers to spend her days lounging among the three different beds/couches that her family has gifted her. She enjoys a walk or two around the neighborhood, as long as she can pretend that she doesn't see any of the other animals to avoid the embarrassment of not wanting to admit she has no real courage to chase them.


At night just as the rest of the family is ready to relax, Lilo suddenly wants to release all her energy. She will place her toys on a mini couch and frantically drag the couch around, giving her toys "a ride." There is also a lot of rolling. Lots and lots of rolling.

Lovable Lexi

Lexi is a 6 year old Mudhol Hound, and she's quite the girl. She's got this beautiful orange coat and eyes that just seem to understand everything. Lexi moves with this smooth agility, making her a perfect example of what a Mudhol Hound should be quick and graceful.


But beyond her looks, Lexi's got a heart of gold. She's not just a great athlete; she's like one of the family. Lexi's got this incredible sense of smell, and she's always ready for some fun. She's just a normal, lovable dog, but there's something special about the way she carries herself a mix of elegance and charm.

Reckless Rio

Reckless Rio, the Mudhol Hound, is a force of nature, embodying an adventurous spirit that's impossible to contain. Her movements are animated and unrestrained, reflecting a zest for life that defines her character. Rio's vibrant and energetic nature makes every moment with her an exhilarating experience.


As a Mudhol Hound, she possesses the innate agility and speed, making her a captivating and dynamic presence. With an undeniable sense of flair, Rio is not just a dog; she's a vivacious friend, bringing an exciting and fearless energy to every corner of life.
